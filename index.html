What Kind of Designer Am I?

User Experience

UX can be a fuzzy term these days. It includes many things, like architecture, interface, interaction, and strategy. Products require touch points with user experience designers at many stages. I keep user needs in mind through the product design lifecycle, while expanding on ideas and discovering new ones through research whenever possible.

Product

Users are important, but so are keeping the lights on. While I firmly believe that what's best for the user is best for the product, there is always a bottom line. Finding the overlap between user needs and business needs is key. As a product designer, I think about implementation, processes, and product innovation.
